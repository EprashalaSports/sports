World-Class Facilities

Players don’t just play here — they live cricket, wake up to nature, and practice on professional pitches. Perfect for professional matches, tournaments, boot camps, and match simulations.

  • BCCI-standard pitches & 60m boundary
  • Full size solo ground (Bigger than Wankhede stadium!)
  • Dedicated practice zones
  • Clean mountain air & scenic valley views on a mountaintop
  • Located 3km near Umbermali Railway Station (Kasara/Shahapur)

Luxury Stay & Upcoming Upgrades

After a rigorous match, relax in comfort. Our ground is perfectly equipped for team tours and weekend cricket getaways.

  • 21 luxury European cottages & grand pavilion available for teams
  • Dedicated dining areas with delicious and hygenic food
  • Coming Soon this Season: Flood lights for night matches!
  • Play, eat, drink, and sleep cricket right on the field!

Refresh & Recover

We know how intense a game of cricket can get, especially under the sun. That's why we've designed the perfect cooldown experience for our athletes.

  • Take a dip in the pool for the tiring cricketers to cool down after hard days of play.
  • Relax your muscles and rejuvenate before the next match.
  • Enjoy the beautiful surrounding mountain views while you rest.

Upcoming Fanpark Experience

We are coming up with a fanpark setup with flood lights for the upcoming cricket season. Now you can enjoy night cricket and a giant 300 inches screen on the ground itself, stay in the triangular ultra modern tents or our newly constructed Air conditioned pavilion on the ground itself.

Never miss your favorite cricket match whether it be IPL or the world cup, enjoy it on the ground itself!

Bookings Open From 1st October Every Year!
