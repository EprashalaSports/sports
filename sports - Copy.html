World-Class Facilities

Players don’t just play here — they live cricket, wake up to nature, and practice on professional pitches. Perfect for practice matches, tournaments, boot camps, and match simulations.


  • BCCI-standard pitches & 60m boundary
  • Full size ground (Bigger than Wankhede stadium!)
  • Dedicated practice zones
  • Clean mountain air & scenic valley views on a mountaintop
  • Located near Umbermali Railway Station (Shahapur / Kasara Ghat)

Luxury Stay & Upcoming Upgrades

After a rigorous match, relax in comfort. Our ground is perfectly equipped for team tours and weekend cricket getaways.


  • 10 luxury European cottages available for teams
  • Swimming pool & dedicated dining areas
  • Coming Soon this Season: Flood lights for night matches!
  • Coming Soon: Fan park and outdoor tents
  • Play, eat, drink, and sleep cricket right on the field!
